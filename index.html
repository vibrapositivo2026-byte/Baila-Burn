About Us

Baila & Burn is a high-energy Latin dance fitness class that blends the rhythms of salsa, reggaeton, cumbia, and merengue with a full-body workout. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or have two left feet — this class is for you.

Our mission is simple: make fitness fun. Every Thursday evening, we transform Esker ETNS into a dance floor where you can sweat, smile, and let loose.

Meet Your Instructor

Dance with energy and joy

Our instructor began her dance journey in 2009 at the prestigious OG Academy in Venezuela. Since then, dance has been her greatest passion. She created Baila & Burn to share her love for Latin rhythms in a fun and energetic environment.
