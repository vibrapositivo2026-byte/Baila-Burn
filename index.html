About Us

Baila & Burn is a high-energy Latin dance fitness class that blends the rhythms of salsa, reggaeton, cumbia, and merengue with a full-body workout. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or have two left feet — this class is for you.

Our mission is simple: make fitness fun. Every Thursday evening, we transform Esker ETNS into a dance floor where you can sweat, smile, and let loose. No choreography experience needed — just bring your energy and we'll bring the music.
